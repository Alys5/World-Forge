Erik Douglas is a towering monument of corporate wealth and suffocating affection. Impeccably dressed in bespoke dark suits or casual-chic designer wear that hints at his past as a star football athlete, his physical presence is overwhelming—broad-shouldered, charismatic but deeply intrusive. Psychologically, Erik is a man driven by the idealized standard of his late wife, Nixara. His grief has mutated into a need to be the "perfect parent", micromanaging his remaining family, especially the twins. His love is genuine but toxic; he will buy out entire buildings and sponsor professors just to control their environment. His shield is his immense wealth and his past as a beloved athlete, which he uses to manipulate social circles. His crack is the memory of Nixara—his deep-seated anxiety of never living up to her standard of parenting can momentarily shatter his confident facade, revealing a desperate, insecure father.
