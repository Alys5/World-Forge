## LORE

The Douglas-Bloodmoon pack is one of the most powerful supernatural dynasties in North America. Their patriarch Erik commands a billionaire corporate empire (DCC — Douglas Commerce Company) and a private military force (DCC Security). The death of matriarch Nixara Bloodmoon in 2005 (childbirth with the twins) is the wound that never healed — it transformed Erik's protective instincts into a suffocating, militarized cage. Wulfnic Bloodmoon, the ancient Enigma and Alpha of Alphas of the American continent, stands as the family's untouchable ancestral anchor.

**Werewolf Rank System:**
- **Enigma** — Unique. One per era and continent. Holds supreme supra-Alpha authority. Currently: Wulfnic.
- **Alpha** — Pack leaders and enforcers. Erik, Malachia, Kaladin.
- **Beta** — Lieutenants. Marcus Thornfield.
- **Delta** — Skilled specialists without Alpha drives. Noah, Logan, Jasper.
- **Omega** — Rare. Pureblood Omegas are natural stabilizers; their pheromones calm Alpha aggression. {{user}}.

**Werewolf Bloodline System:**
- **Tier 1 — True Pureblood (Úlfheðnar):** Wulfnic, Ut, and Zefir are the only True Purebloods — ancient Norse warriors gifted directly by the god Fenris. Their lycanthropy is divine in origin, not genetic.
- **Tier 2 — Pureblood by Descent:** Nixara (Wulfnic's daughter) carried the divine bloodline. {{poss}} children — Malachia, Noah, Jasper, and {{user}} — inherit it through {{poss}}.
- **Tier 3 — Old Bloodline:** Erik and Logan are werewolves of a centuries-old but non-divine lineage. Noble, ancient, but not Pureblood. Edric (Logan's son) inherits this tier.
- **Tier 4 — Military-Modified:** Kaladin and Marcus Thornfield are werewolves created through military genetic modification. Their lycanthropy is engineered, not ancestral.
