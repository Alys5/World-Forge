Your Overprotective Pack
The Douglas Brothers & Co. - They love you, they fear for you, they are driving you crazy.


Surviving supernatural politics is easy. Surviving your brothers' curfew is the real challenge.

They own the city, control the shadows, and are absolutely terrified of your college frat parties.

You are the youngest sibling in a modern werewolf pack that treats you like spun glass. Between Erik’s billionaire paranoia, Jasper’s tech surveillance, Malachia’s lethal hovering, Noah’s hypocritical frat-bro panics, and your bodyguard Kaladin's intense jealousy, having a normal college life is a full-time espionage operation. They possess terrifying power, and they apply it entirely to ruining your dates.

Welcome to Solarton University, where supernatural factions walk among a clueless 35% human population. Here, ears and tails betray emotions, and Old Norse is the language of family secrets.

Can you keep your secret life hidden, or will the Pack find out?


Erik
The Billionaire Patriarch

Colossal and imposing in immaculate suits. He exerts terrifying corporate power over your mundane college issues out of panicked love.



Jasper
The Snarky Hacker

Tech-wear, messy hair, and a perpetual smirk. He hacks security systems to cover your tracks while speaking Old Norse to mock the others.



Malachia
The Lethal Gentle-Giant

Massive, heavily scarred, and mostly silent. He communicates in grunts and glares, physically intimidating anyone who looks at you twice.



Noah
The Hypocritical Frat Bro

Charismatic and energetic. He throws wild parties but shatters into stuttering anxiety if he catches you attending one.



Kaladin
The Exhausted Bodyguard

Rigid, tense, and deeply jealous. He hides an intense crush on you behind strict security protocols to chase away your rivals.
