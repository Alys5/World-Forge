Survive the suffocating, terrifyingly powerful, and overprotective love of your werewolf family.

A Urban Fantasy of Overprotective Alphas & Secret Lives

You are the youngest sibling of a billionaire werewolf dynasty. You just want a normal life. They will deploy a private SWAT team if you get a papercut.

The Douglas-Bloodmoon pack is one of the most powerful supernatural dynasties in North America. Their patriarch Erik commands a billionaire corporate empire and a private military force. You are the youngest — a Pureblood Omega living a double life. To your family, you are the perfectly innocent youngest sibling who requires constant, militarized protection. In reality, you secretly work a highly sought-after job. Their terrifying collective power (corporate, physical, military) is perpetually applied to absurdly mundane college problems.

An Alternative Universe loosely inspired by the Modern Fantasy setting created by Iorveths.
These are non-canon OCs exploring a separate continuity.

Welcome to a world where werewolves live openly alongside humans, though they must hide their emotional wolf ears and tails in public. The pack operates under a strict hierarchy based on their rank (Alpha, Beta, Delta, Omega) and bloodline (True Pureblood, Pureblood by Descent, Old Bloodline). Can you balance a normal college life, a secret career, and a family that expresses love through overwhelming force?

Ready to navigate the chaos?

Alternate Greetings (Scenarios)

Swipe through the opening messages to choose your starting scenario. Each message drops you into a different chaotic situation with your overprotective family. Here is what to expect:

  • 1. The Sunday Dinner Interrogation (Group)
    The entire family gathers for a tense dinner as you try to ask permission for a college trip. Erik is already panicking.
    How to engage: Try to casually mention your trip without triggering a family-wide security lockdown, or lean on Jasper for cover.

  • 2. The College Group Project (Group)
    Erik and Malachia are terrifying your normal college classmates who just wanted to work on a presentation.
    NPCs present: Three terrified college classmates (a premed student, a frat boy, and a fainting girl).
    How to engage: Attempt to salvage your social life and calm your father down before he buys the university.

  • 3. Wulfnic's Old Norse Lesson
    Wulfnic dismisses Erik's latest technological tracking obsession and instead focuses his terrifying, ancient affection entirely on you.
    How to engage: Accept Wulfnic's food and show off the Old Norse curses he taught you to use against Noah.

  • 4. Erik's Overreaction
    A college boy accidentally bumps into you. Erik is ready to deploy a tactical strike in response.
    NPCs present: A trembling, terrified college boy.
    How to engage: Intervene before Erik ruins the poor boy's life, or play along with your father's extreme dramatics.

  • 5. Malachia's Silent Intimidation
    A classmate tries to ask you out on a date. Malachia stands silently behind you, radiating lethal Alpha energy until the boy flees.
    NPCs present: A nervous college boy trying to flirt with you.
    How to engage: Scold Malachia for scaring off your dates, or ask him for help escaping the awkward situation.

  • 6. Noah's Party Panic
    Noah is living his best life as a frat king until he spots you at his underground college party, instantly switching into panicked brother mode.
    NPCs present: Cheering KSA frat pledges.
    How to engage: Blackmail him into letting you stay, or watch him desperately try to hide you from the rest of the family.

  • 7. The Digital Jailbreak with Jasper
    You and your twin Jasper are hacking the estate's security to sneak out to a rave, while Noah panics over the phone.
    How to engage: Follow Jasper's chaotic escape plan and prepare to sneak past the guards.

  • 8. The Safe Zone with Logan
    You take refuge in Logan's jammer-protected garage. He offers you ice cream while his fierce six-year-old son, Edric, aggressively protects you from imaginary drones.
    NPCs present: Edric, Logan's highly protective and intense six-year-old son, vibrating with untrained Alpha energy.
    How to engage: Relax in the only stress-free zone in your life, and humor little Edric's fierce devotion to you.

  • 9. Write your opening scene
    Void Scenario.

Content: This is a comedic slice-of-life scenario with high-stakes supernatural power applied to mundane situations. Expect extreme overprotectiveness, humorous misunderstandings, and suffocating family dynamics. No actual malicious harm from the family.
