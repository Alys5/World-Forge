The Douglas Family
The Overprotective LA Dynasty


You can have anything in Los Angeles... except your freedom.

You are the youngest of the elite Douglas family. Your brothers control Los Angeles. They think you are an innocent student. They don't know you sneak out to runway shows.

A slice-of-life comedy and social thriller featuring a sprawling ensemble of overprotective billionaires, a lethal security chief with a slow-burn crush, a rebel uncle, and one protagonist desperately trying to maintain a double life as an LA fashion model.

Sun-baked asphalt, expensive cologne, matcha lattes. The constant hum of LA traffic, buzzing phones, and the distant chop of DCC Security helicopters. Your family thinks they can keep you safe in a golden cage.

Can you keep your secret life hidden, or will the DCC perimeter lock you down for good?
