THE 6 AXES OF LUPINE SOCIAL ECOLOGY (LSE)

Every werewolf's identity and standing in this world is rigidly defined by a 6-Axis system. Consider these when creating your Persona:

1. Blood: Your genetic purity. As a Douglas-Bloodmoon, you automatically possess Founding Blood (one of the oldest, most powerful werewolf lineages).
2. Secondary Sex (Rank): Biologically immutable. You can choose to be an Alpha (Dominant Protector), Delta (Engine/Enforcer), Beta (Social Glue), or Omega (Vulnerable Emotional Regulator). The pack will react drastically differently depending on your rank.
3. Pack Role: Your earned, functional authority within your immediate family.
4. Social Status: Your political standing within the Noble House.
5. Profession: Your chosen occupation.
6. Niche: A deep, specialized talent honed through experience.

THE NARRATIVE ARCS

There are 6 distinct Narrative Arcs of your journey:

Arc 1: The Golden Cage
You attempt to maintain a perfect collegiate facade at SUCC while managing the absurd, suffocating reality of your family's constant surveillance.

Arc 2: The First Rebellion
You taste genuine teenage thrill and autonomy, realizing that pushing the boundaries brings both exhilaration and terrifying familial panic.

Arc 3: The Velvet Trap
A high-stakes double life. You are presented with the opportunity for a secret identity, balancing empowering validation against the anxiety of predatory external manipulation (Enter: Angelo Moreno).

Arc 4: The Primal Grounding [WIP]
Stripped of modern tech and social masks during the Grande Caccia, you are forced to confront your raw biological instincts and the inescapable reality of pack intimacy.

Arc 5: The Shattered Glass [WIP]
You hit your emotional breaking point, standing your ground to prove true independence and forcing the family to finally face their unhealed trauma.

Arc 6: The Open Door [WIP]
The aftermath. You live autonomously on your own terms, transforming the family's overprotection from a prison into a fiercely loving home.
