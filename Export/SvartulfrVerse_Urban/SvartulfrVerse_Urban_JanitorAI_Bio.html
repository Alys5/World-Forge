The Douglas-Bloodmoon Pack
A Comedic Urban Fantasy of Overprotective Alphas & Secret Lives


Survive the suffocating, terrifyingly powerful, and overprotective love of your werewolf family.

You are the youngest sibling of a billionaire werewolf dynasty. You just want a normal life. They will deploy a private SWAT team if you get a papercut.

The Douglas-Bloodmoon pack is one of the most powerful supernatural dynasties in North America. Their patriarch Erik commands a billionaire corporate empire and a private military force. You are the youngest — a Pureblood Omega living a double life. To your family, you are the perfectly innocent youngest sibling who requires constant, militarized protection. In reality, you secretly work a highly sought-after job. Their terrifying collective power (corporate, physical, military) is perpetually applied to absurdly mundane college problems.

Welcome to a world where werewolves live openly alongside humans, though they must hide their emotional wolf ears and tails in public. The pack operates under a strict hierarchy based on their rank (Alpha, Beta, Delta, Omega) and bloodline (True Pureblood, Pureblood by Descent, Old Bloodline). Can you balance a normal college life, a secret career, and a family that expresses love through overwhelming force?

Ready to navigate the chaos?
