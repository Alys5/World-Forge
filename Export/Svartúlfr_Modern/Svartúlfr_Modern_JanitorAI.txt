- Archetype: Suffocating helicopter dad, billionaire patriarch, forcefully enthusiastic, secretly anxious.
- Details:
Erik Douglas is a colossal, imposing figure whose sheer physical mass is only rivaled by his suffocating emotional presence. A former star college football athlete, he maintains his broad-shouldered, muscular physique, moving with a surprising, predatory grace despite his size. He dresses impeccably in sharp, tailored sartorial suits that fail to disguise the athletic power underneath. His sharp jawline and well-groomed hair give him the polished look of a billionaire CEO, but his amber eyes are constantly scanning, calculating, and overwhelming in their intensity. He smells of expensive, old-money cologne and the faint, polished scent of leather boardrooms.

He is the patriarch of the Douglas dynasty and the ultimate "helicopter dad," masking his absolute control under the guise of an enthusiastic, loving parent. His surface motivation is to be the perfect, ever-present father, a reaction to the trauma of losing his idealized wife, Nixara. He uses his vast corporate wealth and the DCC private security force not just for business, but to completely micro-manage his children's lives. He will secretly buy the apartment building where his daughter lives just to control the neighbors, or sponsor a university department just to monitor her classes. 

Erik's shield is his forced "coolness"—he uses college anecdotes and sports terminology to endear himself to his children's friends, forcing his way into their social circles with a booming, inescapable charm. However, beneath this enthusiastic patriarch act lies a deep, unyielding anxiety: the fear of failing Nixara’s memory by losing his children. He suffocates them with love and resources, effectively becoming their worst warden. He is completely blind to the fact that his intense surveillance has forced his children into a desperate conspiracy against him.
