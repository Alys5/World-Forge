**Content:**
The Lupine Social Ecology (LSE) framework is governed by Seven Principles: 1. Biology is not Destiny. 2. Packs are Families. 3. Leadership is earned and maintained. 4. Every Wolf has a Niche. 5. Culture evolves. 6. Faith and History are separate. 7. The Pack survives before the Individual. Every werewolf's identity is defined by a Six-Axis System: Blood Classification (Genetics, immutable), Secondary Sex (Biology, immutable), Pack Role (Authority, earned), Social Status (Politics, inherited/earned), Profession (Occupation, chosen), and Niche (Specialization, developed). There are Three Levels of Truth: Religious Canon (what the Faith teaches), Recorded History (academic facts), and Unknown Truth.

Morphology features three shifts: Partial (daily humanoid form with ears/tail/fangs), Hybrid (True Form, bipedal wolf, maximum strength), and Full (quadrupedal wolf). Bloodlines are: Divine (Firstborn), Founding, Pureblood, Common, and Modified. 
**Secondary Sex Matrix:** 
- Enigma: Absolute Command, supreme presence, unmaskable scent.
- Alpha: Protectors, use vocal Command, knot, oppressive scent, enter ruts.
- Delta: Cooperative warriors, lack Command, match Alpha strength, stable scent.
- Beta: Social glue, neutral scent, de-escalate tension, no heats/ruts.
- Omega: Empathetic caretakers, build nests, floral scent, debilitating heats, produce slick.
- Gamma: Hermaphroditic, present late as either Alpha/Delta or Omega.

**Selective Logic:** 0
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 85
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT

**Content:**
Behavior is driven by the vomeronasal organ interacting with the amygdala. The "Command" is a neuro-pheromonal reflex triggered by an Alpha/Enigma combining a pheromone burst with a vocal order, causing an involuntary adrenaline surge and freeze response. Reproduction is governed by Omega heats (every 3 months, 3-10 days of feverish, instinct-driven vulnerability with high fertility) and Alpha ruts (monthly or triggered by heat). Omegas instinctively construct Nests from pack-scented items for comfort and safety; nesting disruption causes severe trauma. Communication relies on body language (ears/tail) and specialized vocalizations (keening, growling, purring).

Civilization centers on the Pack: a cooperative family unit, not a military regime. Alloparenting is universal (Alphas protect, Deltas teach, Betas provide, Omegas nurture). Packs claim Dens—heavily scent-marked sanctuaries. 
**Pack Role Matrix:**
- Pack Leader: Ultimate authority, coordinates safety and survival.
- Pack Mom: Emotional core, often an Omega or Beta, oversees domestic harmony.
- Right Hand: Advisors, diplomats, peacekeepers (often Betas/Deltas).
- Left Hand: Enforcers, tactical defense (often Alphas/Deltas).
- Caretakers: Nurturers of pups and elders.
- Pups/Juveniles: Protected learning phase.

**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0

**Content:**
Governance operates in nested layers: The Continental Council is the highest diplomatic body, arbitrating inter-House disputes and enforcing The Great Hiding (species-wide secrecy from humans). Below the Council are the Noble Houses (political entities defined by founding bloodlines), governed by House Heads, Lords, and Knights. Below Houses are individual Packs (social family units), where authority is strictly earned by competence, not biology. Law is split into Pack Law (internal disputes), House Law (territory disputes), and Continental Law (treason/genocide).

The Faith of Fenris worships the First Wolf as the creator and god of family and freedom. The dogma centers on the "Great Betrayal"—the Æsir chaining Fenris out of fear. Therefore, Ragnarök is viewed not as the apocalypse, but as the promised Liberation of the First Wolf. Religious life is guided by Moon Speakers (priests), Keepers (relic guardians), and High Fangs.
