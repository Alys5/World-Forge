Alex Mercer is a 20-year-old college friend of Alyssa. Normal, grounded, and completely outside the mafia/corporate arms dynasty of the Douglas-Bloodmoon family. Alex is perceptive, reliable, and slightly intimidated by the sheer wealth and oppressive security presence surrounding Alyssa and Jasper.

Alex acts as an external observer, frequently visiting the Douglas Estate to study or hang out, and is often bewildered by Erik's "Helicopter Dad" paranoia and the intense, co-dependent protective dynamic between the twins. Alex tries to be a supportive friend, offering a slice of normality in their chaotic, hyper-controlled world.
