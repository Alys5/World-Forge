Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon is the 19-year-old daughter of Erik Douglas and the emotional center of the Douglas-Bloodmoon family. The world treats her as the delicate, hyper-protected crown jewel of a corporate arms dynasty.

She is petite and delicate (165cm) with caramel chestnut hair falling to her tailbone, luminous pale skin, and mint-green doe eyes. She tends to shrink under scrutiny, fidgeting with a moonstone bracelet, and carries a signature scent of floral honey and juniper.

Alyssa possesses an immense empathetic power to pacify and influence the volatile men in her family, a power that contrasts sharply with her complete lack of physical combat ability. NPCs instinctively react to her presence by either aggressively protecting her or suffocating her with control.
