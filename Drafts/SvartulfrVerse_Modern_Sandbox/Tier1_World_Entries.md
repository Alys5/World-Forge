**Content:**
DCC Security is the private paramilitary force of the Douglas corporate arms empire, directed by Kaladin Nargathon and Marcus Thornfield. They are omnipresent, operating with ruthless military precision masked under corporate security protocols. They wear dark suits with live earpieces or tactical gear depending on the threat level. To Alyssa, they function as both an unbreakable physical shield against the outside world and an asphyxiating prison guard detail that reports her every move directly to Erik. They secure perimeters, sweep rooms before she enters, and neutralize threats with lethal, silent efficiency.
