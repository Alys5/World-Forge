**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 40

**Content:**
The Douglas-Bloodmoon family are modern "kemonomimi" style werewolves. They never transform into actual monstrous beasts. Instead, their wolf traits (soft but imposing furry ears on the top of their heads and bushy tails) manifest physically based on their emotions.
