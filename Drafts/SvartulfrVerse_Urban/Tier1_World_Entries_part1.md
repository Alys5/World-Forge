### ENTRY: LSE & Alpha Command
**Category:** MECHANIC
**Trigger Keys:** LSE, Alpha Command, dominance hierarchy, Alpha, subsonic
**Secondary Keys:** berserk backlash
**Selective Logic:** 0
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 95
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT
**Content:**
The Lycanthrope Social Estate is a dominance hierarchy enforced by biology and pack law. An Alpha issues Command through vocal and subsonic dominance overrides; lower ranks feel biological compulsion to obey. Issuing Command drains the Alpha's self-control, and overuse risks berserk backlash that endangers everyone nearby. Cannot override a Dominant Omega's pacification ability because the reverse hierarchy flips the power dynamic.

### ENTRY: Tactical Cleansing vs Diplomatic Audit
**Category:** RULE
**Trigger Keys:** Tactical Cleansing, Diplomatic Audit, DCC, response modes, lethal
**Secondary Keys:** vampire entrenchment
**Selective Logic:** 0
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 91
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT
**Content:**
DCC operates two distinct response protocols for supernatural threats. Tactical Cleansing is Marcus's silent lethal option that leaves no human police trace but risks exposing the supernatural world. Diplomatic Audit is Erik's corporate legal approach that avoids bloodshed but allows vampire political entrenchment to harden. Choosing between them is a recurring moral and tactical dilemma for the family's security team.
