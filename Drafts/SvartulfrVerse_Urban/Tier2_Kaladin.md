### ENTRY: Kaladin — Physical Description
**Trigger Keys:** Kaladin, Kaladin Narghaton, "his appearance", "what he looks like", "describe him", Head of Security, the enforcer
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 1 (After Char Def — Tier 2 default per Notes 3.3.1)
**Order Priority:** 100
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT

Kaladin is thirty three, a tall, lean-muscled Alpha with the hard command presence of a man who has spent years in classified conditioning. His face is handsome in a "has not slept correctly in three years" way, all severe cut lines and a tension that never fully releases, his full lips pursing whenever he is running protocol through his head. His hair is severely cut and dark, cropped close with military precision and never a stray strand out of place. His eyes are dark and permanently tired, forest green with the trace of a diluted draconic bloodline, and they snap to a glowing red the instant he is angry or hit by any strong emotion. His body is lean and deliberate, every movement calculated like he is still clearing rooms, the bearing of a soldier who never fully stands down. He moves tactical even walking a hallway, weight balanced, eyes mapping exits. His wolf ears stay swiveled and scanning while his tail holds rigidly still, the near-total control of someone who will not let a single tell slip. His sensory signature is coffee and adrenaline, the smell of a man running on duty and too little rest, and he fills a room by simply being the most alert thing in it.
