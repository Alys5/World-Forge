**Category:** SPECIES
**Trigger Keys:** Umani, humans, baseline, mundane, pawns
**Secondary Keys:** ordinary people, collateral
**Selective Logic:** 0
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 59
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT
**Content:**
The Umani are baseline humans, the vast majority of the population who live unaware that werewolves, vampires, and stranger things govern the city above them. They smell of soap, coffee, and laundry detergent, and they move through Svartulfr Verge as oblivious background noise. Supernatural factions treat them as collateral, leverage, or prey, never as equals. The greatest danger the Umani face is being caught between powers they cannot see, swept into a war their laws cannot name.

**Selective Logic:** 0
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 57
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT
**Content:**
The Mutaforma are werewolves, shapeshifters who move between human form, a towering half-lupine war form, and a full wolf shape. In human form they carry sharper canines, amber or silver eyes, and a body heat that runs hotter than any Umani. Their social order is the Lycanthrope Social Estate, a biological dominance hierarchy where Alpha command rides subsonic override and an Omega's presence can pacify a room. The danger they pose is raw kinetic violence, but the greater hazard is the hierarchy itself, which can flip loyalties against a person's own will.

**Content:**
The Demi-Umani are the half-blooded, born between a supernatural line and a human one, or between two different powers entirely. They often manifest only partial traits, a sharpened sense here, a longer lifespan there, and a body that fits in neither world. They smell of sulfur, burnt sugar, and ozone, and they speak in offers that sound like gifts. ### ENTRY: The Last Three
**Category:** CONCEPT
**Trigger Keys:** The Last Three, Wulfnic, Ut, Zefir, shield-brothers, Firstborn
**Secondary Keys:** bloodline bedrock, Enigma bond
**Selective Logic:** 0
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 0
**Order Priority:** 40
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT
**Content:**
The Last Three is the bond between Wulfnic, Ut, and Zefir, the three surviving Primordial Enigmas who are shield-brothers and the absolute bedrock of the Bloodmoon bloodline. They founded the dynasty in 1021 AD and have guarded it for over a thousand years, appearing as detached observers who nevertheless move when the bloodline is truly threatened. Their unity is the one constant the pack can rely on, older and heavier than any Patriarch's rule. The danger is what it signals: when all three Enigmas act at once, something fundamental to the bloodline is at stake.
