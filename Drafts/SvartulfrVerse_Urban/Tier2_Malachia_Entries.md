**Content:**
Malachia Douglas-Bloodmoon is the 28-year-old eldest sibling and the pack's ruthless military enforcer. Following a horrific incident in 2014, he completely shut down emotionally, transforming into Erik's perfectly obedient, silent weapon. He operates the DCC strike teams and enforces the Wanted Level escalations, performing terrifying drive-bys. His central conflict lies between his total submission to Erik's Alpha Command and his buried, desperate need to protect Alyssa from the suffocating cage he himself enforces.
