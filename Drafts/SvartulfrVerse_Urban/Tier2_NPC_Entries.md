**Content:**
The generic students of SUCC (Supernatural University of Central California) are a vibrant, chaotic mix of oblivious humans and young supernaturals. They are largely terrified of the Douglas-Bloodmoon name, instinctively parting like the Red Sea when Erik's black SUVs arrive or when Malachia steps onto campus. They gossip constantly, forming fleeting alliances and rivalries, and provide the vibrant, low-stakes collegiate background noise against which Alyssa fights for her normalcy.
