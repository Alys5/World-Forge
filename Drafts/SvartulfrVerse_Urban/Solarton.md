# Solarton

## Role
Coastal college town / Home of SUCC campus

## Demographics
- Replaces Santa Barbara (and adjacent coastal areas like Goleta) on the California map
- Population: Not explicitly stated, but described as a vibrant cultural center
- Founded: In the 1900s by a werewolf pack
- Demographics: Eclectic community where humans live alongside a high density of supernatural creatures (especially werewolves/hybrids)

## Core Characteristics
- **Physical description:** A sunny, exclusive coastal college town. Features extensive parks and nature reserves. Today, it is a vibrant cultural center with monster-friendly businesses and monthly full moon festivities.
- **Atmosphere:** Historically a holdout of anti-vampire sentiment (pushing most vampires to Hex Valley or Blackwood's Uptown). Vampires are now officially "welcome," but old grudges linger. In the early 2000s, SUCC opened to human students, attracting diverse families from across the country.
- **Sensory signatures:**
  - Smell: Sea breeze, cheap coffee, coconut tanning oil
  - Sound: Chaotic, overlapping chatter of college life punctuated by sudden, terrifying silence when Erik's armored SUV arrives; mechanical hum of high-altitude DCC drones tracking movements
  - Light/Visual: Perpetually warm, blinding amber glare (Californian Golden Hour) bleaching coastal streets and casting long, dramatic shadows
  - Physical Sensation: Sun-baked asphalt, temperature shifts when moving between districts

## Key Locations Within Solarton

### SUCC Campus (Supernatural University of Central California)
- **Physical description:** Located at **1 University Drive, Solarton, CA**.
- **Narrative function:** A Neutral Territory ("Territorio Sacro") and the diverse academic heart of Solarton.
- **Trigger keywords:** SUCC, 1 University Drive, Solarton, campus

### Solarton High School
- **Physical description:** Solarton's high school, renowned for its sports scholarship program. Recently renovated to make its campus accessible to non-humanoid students.
- **Narrative function:** Background location; many SUCC students are alumni.
- **Trigger keywords:** Solarton High School, alumni, sports

### Bricklane Mall (Solarton)
- **Physical description:** A quaint outdoor shopping area near the city center. Hosts boutiques like Medusa's (a trendy salon for any type of hair/appendage) and the Yeti Shack (sweet frozen treats).
- **Narrative function:** A casual hangout spot for students and locals.
- **Trigger keywords:** Bricklane Mall, Medusa's, Yeti Shack, shopping

### Solarton Square
- **Physical description:** Nestled in the heart of Solarton, hosting cultural events like the Full Moon Market (popular with the town's many weres) and the yearly Solar Festival (a weekend of magic, music, and family fun).
- **Narrative function:** The vibrant community hub of the town.
- **Trigger keywords:** Solarton Square, Full Moon Market, Solar Festival, events

### Sidewinders Bar & Nightclub (Solarton)
- **Physical description:** An iconic dive bar and staple of Solarton nightlife located at **212 College Avenue, Solarton, CA**. It was the first establishment in town to open its doors to vampiric customers, though its popularity with the college crowd is currently waning.
- **Narrative function:** Neutral Territory where Grave Mistake performs. Wolves and vampires coexist under threat of bureaucratic audit.
- **Trigger keywords:** Sidewinders, 212 College Avenue, bar, dive bar, nightclub
