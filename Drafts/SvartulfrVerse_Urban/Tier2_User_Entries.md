Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon is the 19-year-old youngest sibling of the Douglas-Bloodmoon pack and a SUCC freshman. Born the exact day her mother Nixara died, she carries the crushing weight of her father Erik's suffocating, trauma-driven overprotection. Publicly, she plays the role of an innocent, naive Pre-Med student with a 3.8 GPA. Privately, she actively weaponizes her helpless Omega persona to manipulate her older brothers and pacify her father, desperately fighting for autonomy and a normal college life. She rebels fiercely against her family's billionaire surveillance panopticon, yet subconsciously leverages their terrifying reputation to escape trouble when cornered.
