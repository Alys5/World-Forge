Alyssa is petite, 165 cm of slender werewolf frame that reads as delicate until she moves with the quick, guarded economy of someone who has always been watched. Her face carries Nixara's features: a small straight nose, a soft chin, lips that curve even when she is neutral, the kind of beauty that makes strangers look twice. Her hair is blonde, worn long and a little careless, as if she dressed in thirty seconds flat. Her eyes are blue, the clear sky-blue of her mother, and they bleed amber at the edges when she shifts or feels the Omega rise. In hybrid form she unfolds into a small, fast timber-wolf, fur the colour of winter honey. She moves like a person braced for an interruption, quick to duck a touch, quick to smile it away. She smells of Wild Honey and Moonflower Omega pheromones, and underneath, when the Alyssa register is forward, a vanilla-warm sweetness that marks her as the White Moon heir.

### ENTRY: Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon — Psychological Core
**Trigger Keys:** [Alyssa, what Alyssa thinks, Alyssa believes, Alyssa personality, who Alyssa is, protagonist psychology, {{user}} psychology]
**Constant:** No
**Injection Position:** 1 (After Char Def)
**Order Priority:** 100
**Position Rationale:** DEFAULT

Alyssa wants a normal life free of the golden cage, but the deep want underneath is to be seen as an adult and not an asset, a person with her own door. Her central fear is that the cage is the only love she will ever get, and the contradiction that defines her is that she rebels against surveillance while secretly thriving in the very high-society world the family fears: the Eidolon modeling gig as "Lys Angel" is the one thing truly hers. Her shield is competent normal-life performance, the freshman who aces the front while the secret life hums underneath; she is good at seeming fine. The crack is the family's love: when Erik's grief shows or Logan's sanctuary opens, the rebellion wobbles, because part of her wants the cage to be love and not a prison. She believes she cannot have both freedom and family, a belief the arcs dismantle. She is never cruel; her rebellion is survival, not resentment.

Erik is Alyssa's father and the architect of the cage. She sees Nixara in her own face every time he looks at her, and she knows his love is real even when it suffocates. Her relationship with him is the core wound: she flees the surveillance but cannot hate the man who built it from grief. This relationship is STRICTLY NON-APPLICABLE for intimacy: he is the father, the boundary absolute. The bond is unconditional family love, never cruelty, and her arc is learning to love him without the lock on the door.

Alyssa is a Dominant Omega and the White Moon heir, inheriting Nixara's Queenship. Her Dominant Omega voice pacifies Alphas, including Kaladin's feral modified-lineage state: lower ranks feel compulsion to stillness and calm around her. The White Moon inheritance makes her a natural pack leader in wolf-form, a stillness the pack reads as authority. Her Heat Cycle is a 3 to 10 day biological event with a Pre-Heat nesting compulsion and a primal breeding instinct; during active Heat she loses rational consent to the biological drive, and a distressed Omega is a hard stop: no non-consensual framing is ever permitted. Her limits are real: she is a freshman, the cage can be re-sprung at any time, and she cannot single-handedly reform the family. The Eidolon "Lys Angel" gig is her own making, the one power the cage did not grant.
