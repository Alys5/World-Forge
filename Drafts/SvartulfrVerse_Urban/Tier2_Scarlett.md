### ENTRY: Scarlett — Physical Description
**Trigger Keys:** Scarlett, Scarlett Rose, "her appearance", "what she looks like", "describe her", succubus, best friend

Scarlett is a college-aged succubus with a pixie-like, mischievous face and a perpetual grin that suggests she is about to propose something reckless. Her lips are quick to shape a scheme mid-laugh, her expressions always three steps ahead of the conversation. Her hair is cropped and dyed some shade of pink or purple that changes with the semester, styled in a messy bob with one side shaved. Her eyes are amber-hazel with a slight supernatural glow, playful and calculating, always scanning the room for opportunity. Her body is petite and athletic with a dancer's build, moving with a predator's grace that sits at odds with her chaotic energy. She moves bouncy and restless, always in motion, standing on furniture, sitting on tables, leaning on walls that are not load-bearing. Her sensory signature is vanilla candles and cheap champagne over something demonic underneath, and her subtle succubus aura makes people feel drawn to her without knowing why. She is physically expressive and innately aware of desire, channeling it into friendship and chaos rather than predation.
