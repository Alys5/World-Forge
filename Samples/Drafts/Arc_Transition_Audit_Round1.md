Arc 3 entry trigger: "Anna officially moves into the penthouse" — bringing her personal items, preparing Timmy's room.

These are causally linked: the decision to stay (Arc 2 exit) leads directly to the action of moving in (Arc 3 entry). Same narrative thread, two angles. Continuity is intact.

**CHARACTER_STATE continuity (Anna):** PASS with note

Physical: Arc 2 Anna is 3–6 weeks clean, face softened but cheekbones still prominent, "returning pallor," hair clean and brightening, breasts "beginning return toward natural C-cup." Arc 3 Anna is at natural weight, face "completed its return," laugh lines visible, eyes fully saturated, natural C-cup restored. The physical progression from "recovering" to "restored" requires a time gap of at least several weeks to months. The Arc 2→3 time gap is not precisely specified but the physical change is within plausible range.

Psychological: Arc 2 post-revelation Anna is in theological collapse — "cold sweat, the quality of a woman whose childhood nightmares have literalized, fury first, choosing to stay second." Arc 3 Anna is grounded — "shield not gone but no longer the first thing she reaches for. Leads with directness. Has discovered she is capable of anger on behalf of other people. It presents Anna as having arrived, with no residual theological processing, no echoes of the revelation that just shattered her worldview. The Card_Anna system_prompt for Arc 3+ says "The shield is no longer the first thing you reach for" — this is correct but abrupt.

Recognition test: Post-revelation Arc 2 Anna — terrified but choosing to stay — would recognize herself in Arc 3 Anna, but she would be surprised at how far she has come. ## Failures by Severity

### 🔴 Critical — break the narrative arc

**C1: Arc 1 Tier 3 is structurally incomplete — 4 of 7 Master Design beats missing.**

The Tier 3 Arc 1 draft (`Drafts/Tier3_Arc1_Entries.md`) contains only 3 dramatic beat entries (beats 1, 3, and 5). The Master Design (`Drafts/Master_Design.md`, §9.1) specifies 7 beats. Missing beats:
- Beat 2: "Andrei demonstrates surprising restraint."
- Beat 4: "Anna begins to notice the strangeness of Andrei's men."
- Beat 6: "The Black Hand responds to Jack's encroachment with overwhelming, controlled force."
- Beat 7: "By arc's end, Anna has started to feel something for Andrei."

Beats 6 and 7 are directly needed for Arc 1→2 transition continuity. Without beat 6, the narrative jumps from "Jack Finds Out" to Arc 2's "First Real Intimacy" with no bridge showing the Black Hand's response or the cooling of the territorial threat. Without beat 7, Anna's emotional trajectory from survival mode to "started to feel something" is asserted in ARC_STATE exposition but not dramatized.

**C2: Arc 2→3 missing beat — "the morning after she stays."**

Between Arc 2's exit trigger (Anna chooses to stay) and Arc 3's entry (Anna moves in), the immediate aftermath of Anna's decision is entirely elided. This is the emotional fulcrum of the entire narrative: a mortal woman choosing to remain with the Devil after learning his true identity. The first conversation between Anna and Andrei with the supernatural openly acknowledged, the raw unprocessed hours after her choice, the specific quality of the first morning she wakes up knowing — none of this is captured in either arc's beats. The model receives no guidance for rendering these interactions, which will inevitably occur during the Arc 2→3 transition window.

### 🟠 High — produce noticeable seams in extended play

**H1: Arc 1→2 trigger gap — no causal chain between exit and entry triggers.**

Arc 1 exits on "first blood" (violent confrontation with Jack's forces). Arc 2 enters on Anna's "first night sleeping through without withdrawal symptoms." These describe disconnected states. During extended play, the model may struggle to transition from external threat escalation to internal recovery quiet. The missing causal bridge — what happened after first blood, how Anna moved from acute withdrawal to sleeping through the night — is partially addressed by the missing Arc 1 beats (C1 above), but even with those beats restored, a transitional acknowledgment in Arc 2's early ARC_STATE would strengthen the handoff.

**H2: Arc 2→3 Anna psychological leap lacks transitional acknowledgment.**

The jump from post-revelation theological collapse (Arc 2 end) to grounded directness (Arc 3 start) is the largest character shift between any two arcs. While the time gap can accommodate processing, the Arc 3 ANNA_STATE presents Anna as having fully arrived with no residual echoes of the revelation. The Card_Anna system_prompt for Arc 3+ is correct in its directives but the Arc 3 ANNA_STATE should acknowledge the transition — a line noting that "the theological terror has receded but its echo still surfaces in quiet moments" would bridge the gap.

### 🟡 Medium — risks rather than confirmed gaps

**M1: Arc 2 lacks a dedicated penthouse LOCATION entry.**

Arc 1 has a sterile penthouse entry. Arc 3 has a transformed, lived-in penthouse entry. Arc 2 describes the transitional state ("slightly less sterile, slightly warmer") only in ARC_STATE narrative — there is no triggered LOCATION entry for the intermediate penthouse. When the model needs to describe the penthouse in Arc 2, it has only the sterile Arc 1 entry or the lived-in Arc 3 entry available, neither of which matches the intended Arc 2 atmosphere.

**M2: Bubbles Arc 1→2 pivot point occurs off-page.**

Bubbles's shift from indifference to watching — when he first begins regarding Anna as something other than interchangeable human furniture — happens in the Arc 1→2 time gap. The Arc 1 entry has him as purely indifferent; Arc 2 opens with him already watching. While the Arc 2 "thank you" moment renders the middle state well, the specific trigger for his shift is not shown. A brief nod in Arc 2's NPC_SHIFT — "he is not sure when he started watching her, only that he has" — would close this.

**M3: Arc 3→4 Timmy reclamation timeline is compressed.**

Timmy is reclaimed in Arc 4 beat 1 (Confronting Ingrid). The Master Design NPC presence map says Timmy is "Physically present beginning Arc 3 (reclaimed), fully present Arc 4." The Tier 3 Arc 3 entries reference Timmy's room being prepared but do not include his physical presence. The Arc 3 ARC_STATE says "they are preparing Timmy's room" (future-oriented) but this appears as a narrative goal, not an accomplished fact. The discrepancy between Master Design ("present beginning Arc 3") and Tier 3 drafts (room prepared, child not yet present) should be reconciled. This is a minor structural inconsistency between specification and implementation, not a continuity break.
